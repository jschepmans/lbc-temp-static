About us

Hello!

We from Little Bear Crochet started our journey of crocheting in 2010, after encountering beautiful pattern book filled with amigurumi designs. Online patterns, along with many guides, books, and videos, slowly improved our skills.

In 2015 we started designing our own full-size plush animals for the first time: the tall animals series. We started a pattern company called Little Bear Crochets and started to sell and publish them, that was an exciting time!
